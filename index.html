About Me

Frank was born and raised in the Atlanta area and has been in the eye care industry for over 10 years. Frank is passionate about eyewear and serves as the optician at Windsor Eye Care. He helps patients choose the right frame for their style while also meeting their comfort needs. Frank is also highly skilled in manufacturing glasses in our in-house laboratory. Patients love his contagious smile and down to earth personality.
